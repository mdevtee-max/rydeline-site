RYDELINE — Delete Your Account

App: Rydeline (com.mdevtee.rydeline) · Developer: MDevTee

You can request deletion of your Rydeline account and all associated data at any time. Deletion is completed within 30 days.

Option 1: In the app

  1. Open Rydeline and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Choose Delete account and confirm.

Option 2: By email

Send an email to m.devtee@gmail.com with the subject "Rydeline account deletion" from the email address your account is registered with. We will delete your account and confirm by reply.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email address and sign-in data)
  • All synced vehicles, timeline entries, parts and costs
  • All uploaded photos
  • Your public build pages (links stop working immediately)

Data stored only locally on your device is removed by uninstalling the app. Anonymous subscription records held by Google Play are governed by Google's own policies.

See also our Privacy Policy.
