RYDELINE — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026 · App: Rydeline (com.mdevtee.rydeline) · Developer: MDevTee

Overview

Rydeline is a build log for your car. This policy explains what data the app processes, why, and what your choices are. Short version: the app works fully offline without an account; if you create an account, your vehicle data and photos are synced to our cloud backend so you can back them up, access them across devices, and optionally publish a public build page. We do not sell your data, we do not show ads, and we do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Data we process

Without an account: everything you enter (vehicles, timeline entries, costs, photos, preferences) is stored locally on your device only. We receive none of it.

With an account:

  • Email address — used to create and secure your account (sign-in, verification codes).
  • App content you create — vehicle details, timeline entries, costs, part names and photos are synced to our backend (hosted infrastructure provided by OnSpace/Supabase) so your data is backed up and available across devices.
  • Public build pages — if you switch a vehicle to "public", its build page (photos, specs, timeline, parts list — costs only if you enable it) is visible to anyone with the link and may appear in the in-app community feed. You can switch it back to private at any time.

We do not collect your location, contacts, or financial information. We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs.

Purchases

Subscriptions (Rydeline Pro) are processed by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details; Google provides us only with anonymous entitlement information (whether a subscription is active).

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. Data is stored with our hosting provider (OnSpace/Supabase infrastructure) solely to operate the app. Content you explicitly make public (public build pages) is, by your choice, visible to others.

Data retention & deletion

Your synced data is retained while your account exists. You can delete individual vehicles and entries at any time in the app (this also removes them from the cloud). To delete your account and all associated cloud data, use the account deletion option in the app settings or email us — we will complete deletion within 30 days.

Security

All communication between the app and our backend is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Access to private data is protected by authentication and row-level security rules.

Children

Rydeline is not directed at children and is intended for users 18 and older.

Your rights

Depending on your location (e.g. GDPR in the EU), you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Contact us to exercise these rights.
